By completing this enrollment application, I agree to the following:

  • I must keep both Hospital (Part A) and Medical (Part B) to stay in McLaren Health.
  • By joining this Medicare Advantage Plan or Medicare Prescription Drug Plan, I acknowledge that McLaren Health will share my information with Medicare, who may use it to track my enrollment, to make payments, and for other purposes allowed by Federal law that authorize the collection of this information (see Privacy Act Statement below).
  • Your response to this form is voluntary. However, failure to respond may affect enrollment in the plan.
  • The information on this enrollment form is correct to the best of my knowledge. I understand that if I intentionally provide false information on this form, I will be disenrolled from the plan.
  • I understand that people with Medicare are generally not covered under Medicare while out of the county, except for limited coverage near the U.S. border.
  • I understand that when my McLaren Health coverage begins, I must get all of my medical and prescription drug benefits from McLaren Health. Benefits and services provided by McLaren Health and contained in my McLaren Health "Evidence of Coverage" document (also known as a member contract or subscriber agreement) will be covered. Neither Medicare nor McLaren Health will pay for benefits or services that are not covered.

If signed by an authorized representative (as described above), this signature certifies that: 1. This person is authorized under State law to complete this enrollment, and 2. Documentation of this authority is available upon request by Medicare.
